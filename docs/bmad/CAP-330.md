### CAP-330 — Exploration policy (UCB/Thompson on Top‑N)

Goal: Accelerate convergence with calibrated uncertainty-driven exploration while respecting cohort guardrails and A++ gates.

Policy
- Scope: apply to final `topK` from CAP‑320 ranker
- Strategy: support `ucb` and `thompson` modes selectable via request
- Budget: `budgetFraction ∈ [0, 0.3]` of `topK` for exploration (default 0.2)
- Pool: high-uncertainty items within topK plus tail candidates below topK
- Promotion/Demotion: replace last `budget` positions in topK with exploration picks

Confidence and Uncertainty
- Use calibrated CI widths from CAP‑320 heads; larger width ⇒ larger exploration bonus
- Thompson sampling approximated via mean±noise seeded for reproducibility

Cohort Guards
- Hook for per-cohort eligibility: device/locale/category; default allow-all
- Respect regret floors from CAP‑320 (no forced promotion of regret-capped items)

Events (ALG‑101)
- `EVT.Rec.ItemPromoted` and `EVT.Rec.ItemDemoted` with `auditId` and `item_id`
- Maintains `EVT.Rec.ScoreServed` and `EVT.Rec.RegretCapped` from CAP‑320

API wiring
- `POST /api/recs/serve` fields:
  - `strategy`: 'ucb' | 'thompson'
  - `budgetFraction`: 0..0.3
  - `seed?`: number for deterministic replay
  - Returns `explore_id` alongside `alg_version`, `calibration_version`

Acceptance
- −40% impressions-to-lock-in vs baseline
- No material increase in regret while exploration active

Observability
- Counters: promoted, demoted, explore_budget, explore_pool_size
- Record `explore_id` for analysis and replay
