Part I: Understanding Video Ideas - The 7 Idea Legos
Every viral video is constructed from 7 fundamental components:

Topic - One sentence description of video content
Angle - Unique premise, take, and key facts explaining the topic
Hook Structure - Three-part system:

Spoken hook (verbal content)
Text hook (on-screen text)
Visual hook (what's shown and presentation)


Story Structure - Narrative flow (breakdown, tutorial, list, etc.)
Visual Format - Overall layout (green screen, POV, faceless, etc.)
Key Visuals - Actual visuals used (A-roll, B-roll, animations)
Audio - Background music or sound effects
