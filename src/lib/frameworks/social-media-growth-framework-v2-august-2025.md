## Dynamic Percentile System (DPS) Overview

The sophisticated, adaptive method for determining viral content that replaces the outdated "5X rule."

### Core Formula:
```
Viral_Score = (View_Count / Cohort_Median) × Platform_Weight × Decay_Factor
```

Where:
- **Cohort_Median** = median views for accounts with ±20% follower count
- **Platform_Weight** = {TikTok: 1.0, Instagram: 0.85, YouTube: 0.7}
- **Decay_Factor** = e^(-λt), where λ = platform decay rate and t = time

### Viral Classification Thresholds:
- **Viral**: Top 5% of Viral_Score within cohort
- **Hyper-viral**: Top 1% of Viral_Score within cohort
- **Mega-viral**: Top 0.1% of Viral_Score within cohort

## Frameworks 1-19: Core Viral Strategies

### 1. Steal This Genius Viral Strategy - Rating Trend
Quick-fire rating format that leverages fast-paced judgment content for maximum engagement.

**When to Use**: Expert positioning; myth-busting content; product comparisons; advice evaluation; entertainment education; quick viral wins.

**Core Mechanics**:
- Hook templates: "[Expert title] rates [category] on scale of 1-10"
- Structure: Quick rating + brief explanation
- Pacing: 3-5 seconds per item maximum
- Visual: Number appears on screen with item
- Energy: High energy, quick decisions
- Items: 7-10 things to rate

**Success Benchmarks (Updated with DPS)**:
- Viral rate: 40-50% reach top 5% performance relative to creator's cohort (DPS)
- Hyper-viral potential: top 1% performance relative to creator's cohort (DPS) when execution perfect
- Completion rate: >70% due to fast pacing
- Comment rate: 2-3% arguing with ratings
- Share rate: 0.5-1.0%
- Production time: <1 hour total

**Required Assets & Effort**:
- List of items to rate
- Simple filming setup
- One assistant to read items
- Basic editing for captions
- Minimal production complexity

**Notable Risks**: Controversial ratings causing backlash; appearing dismissive of topics; oversimplification of complex subjects; copycat saturation.

**Real-World Examples**:
- "Personal trainer rates weight loss advice"
- "Detox teas: 0, Cold showers: 10"
- "Dermatologist rates skincare trends"

**Optimization Checklist**:
- [ ] Hook clearly states expertise
- [ ] 7-10 items prepared to rate
- [ ] Ratings are definitive
- [ ] Brief explanation for extreme ratings
- [ ] High energy maintained throughout
- [ ] Track >70% completion rate

### 2. Create 70 Viral Content Ideas in 1 Hour Framework
Transform one proven concept into 70 unique video opportunities.

**When to Use**: Content calendar planning; team content creation; maximizing winning concepts; systematic content approach.

**Core Mechanics**:
- Step 1: Find one viral video (top 5% performance relative to creator's cohort - DPS)
- Step 2: Apply 7 angle variations (challenge, tutorial, competition, transformation, educational, myth/mistakes, storytelling)
- Step 3: Apply 10 format variations to each angle (talking back/forth, voiceover, trending audio+B-roll, talking form, reaction/stitch, shot/angle changes, visuals, green screen/split screen, interview, clone)
- Result: 70 unique video options
- Pick best 10-20 to produce

**Success Benchmarks (Updated with DPS)**:
- Content efficiency: 10X improved
- Viral rate: 20-30% of variations achieve top 10% performance relative to creator's cohort (DPS)
- Planning time: Reduced 70%
- Team scalability: Maximum
- Creative burnout: Minimized

**Required Assets & Effort**:
- Initial viral video research
- Creative framework understanding
- Planning discipline
- Team coordination
- High upfront, low ongoing

**Notable Risks**: Over-milking concepts; audience fatigue; missing fresh trends; formulaic content.

**Real-World Examples**:
- One fitness concept → 70 videos
- Business tip expanded systematically
- Educational topic fully explored
- Series planned from single video

**Optimization Checklist**:
- [ ] Source video truly viral (top 5% performance relative to creator's cohort - DPS)
- [ ] All 7 angles explored
- [ ] Format variety maintained
- [ ] Quality standards upheld
- [ ] Audience response monitored
- [ ] Best performers identified

### 3. Big Creators Go Viral and Recreate Framework
Systematically recreate viral videos with small adjustments for consistent success.

**When to Use**: Scaling successful content; building on proven concepts; consistent growth; team content creation.

**Core Mechanics**:
- Monitor successful creators in your niche
- Identify their viral videos (top 5% DPS)
- Recreate with your unique perspective
- Make 20% adjustment: hook, angle, or delivery
- Maintain core viral structure

**Success Benchmarks (DPS)**:
- Hit rate: 40-60% of recreations go viral
- Time savings: 50% vs. original ideation
- Learning curve: Shortened dramatically
- Consistency: High output quality

**Required Assets**:
- Viral video monitoring system
- Quick production capability
- Authentic unique angle
- Basic editing skills

**Notable Risks**: Being seen as copycat; missing authenticity; over-reliance on others' ideas.

**Optimization Checklist**:
- [ ] Source video confirmed top 5% DPS
- [ ] 20% unique adjustment made
- [ ] Authentic voice maintained
- [ ] Credit given when appropriate
